This is a demo of a Yesterday's Weather Chart.

Demo's are also available of Burn Up charts, Long Term Trend charts, Tables and Reports.

  • Yesterday's weather compares work completed iteration to iteration (week to week).
  • You can use labels to break out different types of work to see, for example, how much of your work each week is planned versus unexpected.
  • Use yesterday's weather to predict how much you will get done.
  • Use yesterday's weather to manage down waste.
